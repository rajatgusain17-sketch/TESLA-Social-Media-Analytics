# 🚗 Tesla Social Media Analytics (Excel Project)
## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Tesla's social media performance across multiple platforms using Microsoft Excel. The goal is to derive actionable insights related to engagement, campaign performance, follower growth, and content strategy.
The dataset consists of:      
•📊 Posts Data (Likes, Shares, Comments, Impressions, Hashtags)         
•📈 Engagement Summary (Followers, Ad Spend)     
•📅 Campaign Metadata (Campaign timelines, budgets, objectives)            
## 🎯 Objectives
•Clean and preprocess raw data     
•Analyze engagement metrics across platforms    
•Identify top-performing posts and hashtags     
•Evaluate platform performance and ROI     
•Measure campaign effectiveness     
•Analyze follower growth trends     
•Provide actionable business insights    
## 🧹 Data Cleaning
•Removed duplicate records      
•Standardized date formats and platform names             
•Formatted numeric columns (Likes, Reach, Impressions, Ad Spend)          
•Split hashtags into structured columns            
## 📊 Key Analysis
### 🔹 Engagement Analysis
•Calculated Engagement Rate:     
####  ```Engagement Rate = (Likes + Shares + Comments) / Impressions```     
•Identified Top 10 posts based on engagement
•Analyzed engagement by platform and content type   


### 🔹 Platform Analysis
•Compared engagement across platforms   
•Analyzed Engagement vs Ad Spend   
•Calculated Engagement Efficiency (Engagement per Ad Spend)   


### 🔹 Hashtag Analysis
•Identified most frequently used hashtags    
•Compared performance of posts by hashtag usage   
### 🔹 Campaign Effectiveness
•Measured engagement uplift (Before vs During campaign)      
•Compared campaign ROI (Engagement vs Spend)     
•Identified highest performing campaigns    
### 🔹 Follower Growth Analysis
•Calculated Net Growth:   
####  ```Net Growth = New Followers - Unfollows```              
•Visualized weekly growth trends            
•Applied Moving Average for trend smoothing                
•Analyzed correlation between Ad Spend and Growth              

## 📈 Key Insights
•Instagram and Twitter provide higher engagement efficiency         
•Facebook generates the highest engagement but at higher cost           
•YouTube shows lower engagement despite significant ad spend            
•Campaigns significantly improve engagement rates              
•Sustainability-focused campaigns drive strong follower growth       
•Innovation-related hashtags perform best             
## 🧠 Recommendations
•Focus on Instagram and Twitter for cost-effective engagement            
•Optimize YouTube content strategy instead of increasing ad spend               
•Leverage campaign-based marketing for engagement spikes              
•Use high-performing hashtags consistently             
•Invest in sustainability and innovation messaging
